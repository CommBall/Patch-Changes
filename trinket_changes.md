# Battleground Updates

Dev Comment: Overall, we're very pleased with how Trinkets and our last balance patch have been performing. For this patch, we wanted to take a bit more of a focused, lighter touch. We've made a few adjustments to Murlocs, Elementals, and Quilboar, as well as some specific stand-out cards like Land Lubber and Fire-forged Evoker. For Trinkets, we focused primarily on cost adjustments and curating which trinkets can be offered as �pivot� options. As always, we'll continue to monitor the game and your feedback and make further adjustments as needed.
